Oxygen

Indications

Absent / Inadequate ventilation following an acute medical or traumatic event.
COPD SpO2 < 92%
Adult SpO2 < 94%
Child SpO2 <96%

Contra-Indications

Bleomycin lung injury (cancer treatment)

Oxygen

Side Effects

Prolonged use of O2 with chronic COPD patients may lead to reduction in ventilation stimulus

Dose

100% 15 l/min then titrate to achieve SpO2:
COPD 92% (or check COPD Alert Card)
Adult 94% - 98%
Child 96% - 98%


ASPRIN

Indications

Chest Pain, Suspected MI

Contra-Indications

Age < 16,
Bleeding disorder,
Bleeding ulcer,
Alergy-KSAR

ASPRIN

Side Effects

Bronchospasm,
Epigastric pain/discomfort,
Gastro Intestinal bleeding,
Icreased bleeding time,
Skin hypersensitivity

Dose

300mg Tabled (Disolved in small amount of water or chewed) Repeat -NO-


GLUCOSE GEL

Indications

Hypoglycemia, Known diabetic with confusion or altered levels of consciousness

Contra-Indications

KSAR - Known severe adverse reaction

GLUCOSE GEL

Side Effects

May cause Vommiting in patient <5yo if given too quickly

Dose

>8yo (Adult) 10-20g Buccal @Repeat PRN
≤8yo 5-10g Buccal @Repeat PRN

Contra-Indications

None - Known

Tacyarythmias
Hypertension
